Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in our solar system. It has a thin atmosphere and surface temperatures that can reach up to 430°C during the day and drop to -180°C at night.

  • Diameter: 4,880 km
  • Distance from Sun: 57.9 million km
  • Orbital Period: 88 days
  • Moons: None
