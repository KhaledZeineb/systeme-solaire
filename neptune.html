Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun in our solar system. It is a cold, dark, and windy planet with an atmosphere composed mainly of hydrogen, helium, and methane, which gives it a blue color. Neptune has a series of faint rings and many moons.

  • Diameter: 49,244 km
  • Distance from Sun: 4.5 billion km
  • Orbital Period: 165 years
  • Moons: 14
