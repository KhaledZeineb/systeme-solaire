Uranus

Uranus is the seventh planet from the Sun and is unique due to its tilted rotational axis, which causes it to rotate on its side. It has a cold atmosphere composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, with traces of methane that give it a blue-green color. Uranus also has a faint ring system and many moons.

  • Diameter: 50,724 km
  • Distance from Sun: 2.87 billion km
  • Orbital Period: 84 years
  • Moons: 27
